## Overview

Connect your users to Pipedrive for sales pipeline management, contact tracking, deal management, and activity logging.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `pipedrive` |
| **Category** | CRM & Sales |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Pipedrive OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Pipedrive Developer Hub">
    Navigate to [developers.pipedrive.com](https://developers.pipedrive.com/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new app">
    Go to **Your Apps** and click **Create an app**. Fill in the app name and details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the redirect URI">
    Under **OAuth & Access scopes**, add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **Callback URL** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Pipedrive provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Pipedrive**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Pipedrive Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Pipedrive Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `deals:read` | Read deal data |
| `deals:write` | Create and manage deals |
| `contacts:read` | Read contact and person data |
| `contacts:write` | Create and manage contacts |
| `activities:read` | Read activity data |
| `activities:write` | Create and manage activities |
