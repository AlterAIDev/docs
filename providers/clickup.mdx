## Overview

Connect your users to ClickUp for project management, task tracking, and team collaboration.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `clickup` |
| **Category** | Productivity |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a ClickUp OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to ClickUp Integrations">
    Navigate to [ClickUp Settings > Integrations > ClickUp API](https://app.clickup.com/settings/integrations) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an app">
    Click **Create an App**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your app">
    Enter the app name and add the **Redirect URI** from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) as the **Redirect URL**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add ClickUp provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **ClickUp**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your ClickUp Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your ClickUp Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notes

- ClickUp does not use scopes. The OAuth token grants access based on the user's workspace permissions.
