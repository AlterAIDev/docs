## Overview

Connect your users to Bitly for link shortening, click analytics, and link management.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `bitly` |
| **Category** | Marketing |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Not available |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Bitly OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Bitly Developer Settings">
    Navigate to [Bitly Settings > API > OAuth Apps](https://app.bitly.com/settings/api/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register a new application">
    Click **Register New Application**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your app">
    Fill in the application name, link, and description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the redirect URI">
    Add the **Redirect URI** from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **Redirect URIs** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Bitly provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Bitly**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Bitly Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Bitly Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notes

- Bitly does not use scopes. OAuth tokens have full access to the user's account.
- Bitly tokens do not expire and cannot be refreshed.
