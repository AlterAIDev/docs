## Overview

Connect your users to Snapchat for marketing campaign management, advertising data, and profile information.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `snapchat` |
| **Category** | Social |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Snapchat OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Snapchat Business Manager">
    Navigate to [business.snapchat.com](https://business.snapchat.com/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register your app">
    Go to **Business Settings** > **OAuth Apps** and click **Register App**. Fill in the app name and description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the redirect URI">
    Add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **Redirect URLs** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Snapchat provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Snapchat**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Snapchat Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Snapchat Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `snapchat-marketing-api` | Access to Snapchat Marketing API for ad management |
| `snapchat-profile-api` | Access to user profile data |

## Notes

- Snapchat Marketing API access requires approval from Snapchat. Apply through the Business Manager.
- See the [Snapchat Marketing API authentication documentation](https://marketingapi.snapchat.com/docs/#authentication) for more details.
