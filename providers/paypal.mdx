## Overview

Connect your users to PayPal for payment processing, account information, and financial transaction data.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `paypal` |
| **Category** | Payments |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) |

## Step 1: Create a PayPal OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the PayPal Developer Dashboard">
    Navigate to [developer.paypal.com/dashboard/applications](https://developer.paypal.com/dashboard/applications) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new app">
    Click **Create App**, enter an app name, and select **Merchant** or **Platform** as the app type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the redirect URI">
    Under **App Settings**, add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) to the **Return URL** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterai.dev](https://portal.alterai.dev) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add PayPal provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **PayPal**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your PayPal Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your PayPal Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `openid` | OpenID Connect authentication |
| `email` | Access to verified email address |
| `profile` | Access to account profile information |
| `address` | Access to account address |
| `https://uri.paypal.com/services/paypalattributes` | Access to PayPal account attributes |

## Notes

- PayPal has separate sandbox and production environments. Use sandbox credentials for development.
- See the [PayPal Log In with PayPal documentation](https://developer.paypal.com/docs/log-in-with-paypal/integrate/) for more details.
