## Overview

Connect your users to eBay for marketplace selling, inventory management, order fulfillment, and account data.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `ebay` |
| **Category** | E-Commerce |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create an eBay OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the eBay Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [developer.ebay.com/my/keys](https://developer.ebay.com/my/keys) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new application">
    Click **Get a Keyset** and fill in the application details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the redirect URI">
    In your app settings, add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **RuName** (eBay Redirect URL name) configuration.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** (App ID) and **Client Secret** (Cert ID).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add eBay provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **eBay**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your eBay App ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your eBay Cert ID
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope` | Basic public data access |
| `https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.marketing` | Marketing tools access |
| `https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.inventory` | Inventory management |
| `https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.fulfillment` | Order fulfillment |
| `https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.account` | Account settings |

## Notes

- eBay uses full URL strings as scope values rather than short names.
- eBay has separate sandbox and production environments. Test with sandbox credentials before going to production.
- See the [eBay OAuth documentation](https://developer.ebay.com/api-docs/static/oauth-authorization-code-grant.html) for more details.
