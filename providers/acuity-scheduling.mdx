## Overview

Connect your users to Acuity Scheduling for appointment booking, calendar management, and client scheduling.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `acuity-scheduling` |
| **Category** | Productivity |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create an Acuity Scheduling OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Acuity Scheduling Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [Acuity Scheduling Developer Portal](https://developers.acuityscheduling.com/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register a new application">
    Register a new OAuth2 application.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the application">
    Enter the application name and description. Add the Alter callback URL (from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com)) to the **Redirect URI** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Acuity Scheduling provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Acuity Scheduling**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Acuity Scheduling Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Acuity Scheduling Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `api-v1` | Full access to the Acuity Scheduling API v1 (appointments, calendars, clients) |

<Note>
  The `api-v1` scope is required for API access and is selected by default.
</Note>

## Notes

- Acuity Scheduling is owned by Squarespace. You may need a Squarespace account to access the developer portal.
- The `api-v1` scope provides full access to appointments, calendars, and client data.
- See the [Acuity Scheduling API documentation](https://developers.acuityscheduling.com/reference/oauth2) for more details on available endpoints.
