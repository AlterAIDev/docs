## Overview

Connect your users to Asana for project management, task tracking, and team collaboration.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `asana` |
| **Category** | Productivity |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) |

## Step 1: Create an Asana OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Asana Developer Console">
    Navigate to [app.asana.com/0/my-apps](https://app.asana.com/0/my-apps) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new app">
    Click **Create New App**. Enter your application name and select your workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the redirect URL">
    Under **OAuth**, add the **Redirect URI** from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) to the **Redirect URLs** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterai.dev](https://portal.alterai.dev) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Asana provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Asana**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Asana Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Asana Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `default` | Access to all Asana resources the user can access |

## Notes

- Asana uses a single `default` scope that grants access to all resources the authenticated user has permissions for.
- See the [Asana OAuth documentation](https://developers.asana.com/docs/oauth) for more details on authentication and available endpoints.
