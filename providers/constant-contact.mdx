## Overview

Connect your users to Constant Contact for email marketing, campaign management, and contact list administration.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `constant-contact` |
| **Category** | Marketing |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Constant Contact OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Constant Contact Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [Constant Contact Developer Portal](https://app.constantcontact.com/pages/dma/portal/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new application">
    Click **New Application**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your app">
    Enter the application name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the redirect URI">
    Add the **Redirect URI** from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **Redirect URI** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **API Key** (Client ID) and **App Secret** (Client Secret).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your App Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Constant Contact provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Constant Contact**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Constant Contact API Key
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Constant Contact App Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `contact_data` | Read and write access to contacts and contact lists |
| `campaign_data` | Read and write access to email campaigns |
| `offline_access` | Request a refresh token for long-lived access |

<Tip>
  Include the `offline_access` scope to receive a refresh token. Without it, your token will expire and require the user to re-authorize.
</Tip>

## Notes

- The `offline_access` scope is included by default to enable automatic token refresh.
- Constant Contact refers to the Client ID as "API Key" and Client Secret as "App Secret" in their portal.
