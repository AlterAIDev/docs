## Overview

Connect your users to Calendly for scheduling automation, event management, and availability tracking.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `calendly` |
| **Category** | Productivity |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) |

## Step 1: Create a Calendly OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Calendly Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [developer.calendly.com](https://developer.calendly.com/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an application">
    Click **Create an Application** under **My Apps**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your app">
    Fill in the app name, description, and company URL.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the redirect URI">
    Add the **Redirect URI** from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) to the **Redirect URI** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterai.dev](https://portal.alterai.dev) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Calendly provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Calendly**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Calendly Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Calendly Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `default` | Access to Calendly scheduling data including events, invitees, and availability |

## Notes

- Calendly OAuth apps require approval for production access.
- During development, you can test with up to 100 users.
