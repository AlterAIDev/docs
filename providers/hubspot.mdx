## Overview

Connect your users to HubSpot for CRM data, contact management, deal tracking, and marketing automation.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `hubspot` |
| **Category** | Popular |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) |

## Step 1: Create a HubSpot OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the HubSpot Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [developers.hubspot.com](https://developers.hubspot.com/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new app">
    Go to **Apps** and click **Create app**. Fill in the app name and details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure OAuth settings">
    Under the **Auth** tab, add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterai.dev) to **Redirect URLs**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterai.dev](https://portal.alterai.dev) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add HubSpot provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **HubSpot**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your HubSpot Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your HubSpot Client secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `crm.objects.contacts.read` | Read contact records |
| `crm.objects.contacts.write` | Create and update contacts |
| `crm.objects.companies.read` | Read company records |
| `crm.objects.companies.write` | Create and update companies |
| `crm.objects.deals.read` | Read deal records |
| `crm.objects.deals.write` | Create and update deals |

## Notes

- HubSpot enforces granular scopes — request only what your app needs.
- Tokens expire after 6 hours. Alter Vault handles automatic refresh using the refresh token.
- See the [HubSpot OAuth quickstart](https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/oauth-quickstart-guide) for more details.
