## Overview

Connect your users to Basecamp for project management, team collaboration, and task tracking.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `basecamp` |
| **Category** | Productivity |
| **PKCE** | Supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Basecamp OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 37signals Launchpad">
    Navigate to [37signals Launchpad](https://launchpad.37signals.com/integrations) and sign in with your Basecamp account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register a new application">
    Click **Register another application**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the application">
    Enter the application name and company website. Add the Alter callback URL (from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com)) to the **Redirect URI** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Basecamp provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Basecamp**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Basecamp Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Basecamp Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

Basecamp does not use OAuth scopes. The access token grants access based on the authenticating user's permissions within their Basecamp account.

## Notes

- The OAuth flow goes through 37signals Launchpad, the parent company of Basecamp.
- Basecamp does not use scopes. Tokens grant access to all Basecamp resources the authenticating user has permission to access.
- See the [Basecamp API documentation](https://github.com/basecamp/api/blob/master/sections/authentication.md) for more details on authentication and available endpoints.
