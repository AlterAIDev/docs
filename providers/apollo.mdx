## Overview

Connect your users to Apollo.io for sales intelligence, lead enrichment, and engagement sequences.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `apollo` |
| **Category** | CRM & Sales |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Automatic |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create an Apollo.io OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Apollo.io Developer Portal">
    Navigate to [Apollo.io Developer Portal](https://developer.apollo.io/) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new application">
    Click **Create Application**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the application">
    Enter the application name and description. Add the Alter callback URL (from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com)) to the **Redirect URI** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Client Secret publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Apollo.io provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Apollo.io**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Apollo.io Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Apollo.io Client Secret
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `api:default` | Standard access to Apollo.io API for contacts, accounts, and sequences |

## Notes

- Apollo.io uses a single `api:default` scope that provides standard access to the API, including contacts, accounts, and sequences.
- See the [Apollo.io API documentation](https://apolloio.github.io/apollo-api-docs/) for more details on available endpoints.
