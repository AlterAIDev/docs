## Overview

Connect your users to Stripe via Stripe Connect for payment processing, account management, and financial data access.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Provider ID** | `stripe` |
| **Category** | Payments |
| **PKCE** | Not supported |
| **Token refresh** | Not supported (permanent tokens) |
| **Redirect URI** | Shown in [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) |

## Step 1: Create a Stripe Connect OAuth App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Stripe Connect OAuth Settings">
    Navigate to [dashboard.stripe.com/settings/connect/onboarding-options/oauth](https://dashboard.stripe.com/settings/connect/onboarding-options/oauth) and sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable OAuth">
    Enable **Onboard accounts with OAuth**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the redirect URI">
    Add the Alter callback URL from the [Developer Portal](https://portal.alterauth.com) to the **Redirect URIs** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your credentials">
    Copy the **Client ID** from the Connect settings page. Your **Secret Key** from the **Developers** > **API keys** page is your Client Secret.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Never share your Secret Key publicly. Store it securely and only enter it in the Alter Vault Developer Portal.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Add to Alter Vault

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Developer Portal">
    Go to [portal.alterauth.com](https://portal.alterauth.com) and navigate to your app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Stripe provider">
    Go to **OAuth Providers** > **Add Provider** > **Stripe**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - **Client ID**: Paste your Stripe Connect Client ID
    - **Client Secret**: Paste your Stripe Secret Key
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select scopes">
    Choose the scopes your app needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The provider is now active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `read_only` | Read-only access to the connected account |
| `read_write` | Full read and write access to the connected account |

## Notes

- Stripe Connect tokens do not expire. There is no refresh token flow.
- Stripe Connect is for connecting to other Stripe accounts (not individual user accounts).
- Stripe recommends Connect Onboarding instead of OAuth for new integrations.
- See the [Stripe Connect OAuth reference](https://docs.stripe.com/connect/oauth-reference) for more details.
